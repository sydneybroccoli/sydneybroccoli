**OPEN FOR WORK**

Born and raised in the suburbs of Seattle, WA.

Received a Bachelor of Science in Bioelectrical Engineering (with a minor in Electrical Engineering) from Marquette University in 2017. Participated in a variety of organizations including: MU Women's Club Lacrosse, Alpha Chi Omega Women's Fraternity and the Les Aspin Center for Goverment.

I interned with the Food & Drug Administration in the Human Device Interaction Lab. My work consisted of research and analysis of testing methods related to upper extremity prosthetic devices.

Recently, I attended and completed the Le Wagon Coding Bootcamp in Bali, Indonesia where I learned a variety of font- and back-end languages over the course of 9-weeks. Since completing the bootcamp, I have been continuing to improve and expand my coding skills through a number of collaborative and personal projects.
